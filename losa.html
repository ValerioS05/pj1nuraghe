Nuraghe Losa

The Losa Nuraghe is one of the most significant examples of this type of structure. It is situated near the town of Abbasanta, in the province of Oristano, in central Sardinia. The site consists of a central tower surrounded by a complex arrangement of other structures, including a courtyard, additional towers, and a village area. The central tower of the Losa Nuraghe is particularly well-preserved, standing at approximately 14m. in height. Overall, the Losa Nuraghe stands as a testament to the ingenuity and cultural richness of the ancient Nuragic civilization, offering valuable insights into the island's prehistoric past.
